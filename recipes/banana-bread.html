Banana Bread

Time to use up those bananas you've left on the counter for weeks

Easy recipe? Yes. Delicious? Also yes.

Adapted from Elise Bauer's recipe on simplyrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 2-3 ripe bananas (frozen & thawed works too)
  • 1⁄3 butter (melted)
  • 1⁄2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3⁄4 sugar (or less, to taste)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1⁄2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees (F). Butter an 8 x 4 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas until there are no large pieces left. Mix in the melted butter.
  3. Mix in the remaining ingredients. (Using a fork works fine)
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 55 to 60 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Flip onto a cooling rack and let sit for 5 minutes. Enjoy.
